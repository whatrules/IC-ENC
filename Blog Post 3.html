Log in to your IC-ENC Account

by Kevin Black at 16:57 in Technical, IC-ENC Suggests


​To log in to your IC-ENC account (Hydrographic Offices (HOs) or Value Added Resellers (VARs)) please follow the process detailed below:

Click on the relevant section of the login area, ie. Hydrographic Offices or VARs.

You will be redirected to the Office365 generic login page. Select the 'Sign in with a Microsoft account' option.

On the sign in screen login with your new username and your existing password.

For VARs please use your existing username suffixed with @outlook.com. If your original username did not begin with 'VAR' then please add 'VAR' as a prefix and @outlook.com as a suffix. This will result in a VAR username of VARxxx@outlook.com.

For HOs please use your existing username with the prefix 'ENC' and the suffix '@outlook.com'.​ Doing so will provide you with a new usernameENCxxxx@outlook.com.

Tick the 'keep me signed in' box.



If you have any issues with logging in to your account please contact

therenc@ic-enc.org
