Suriname becomes 29th member of IC-ENC

by Kevin Black at 10:13 in Announcements




IC-ENC has great pleasure in announcing that Suriname has now formally joined. This is the 29th nation to join IC-ENC's ever increasing family of member Hydrographic Offices.

Suriname has four ENCs which are produced in co-operation with the Netherlands Hydrographic Office. IC-ENC will be supporting the Maritime Authority of Suriname (MAS) to develop its own ENC production capability. In time, MAS will take over future production and maintenance of the ENCs in this region.
