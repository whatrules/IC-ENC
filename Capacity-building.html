Capacity Building

IC-ENC believes it is important to continue to develop its ability to provide a valuable service to its members. This was fully supported by the Steering Committee in 2013, where approval was given to increase the level of capacity building work. There are currently three ongoing capacity building projects:

  • Regional Office Concept
  • International Validation training course
  • External accreditation
