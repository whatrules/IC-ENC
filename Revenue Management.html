ENC Revenue Management

IC-ENC manages all aspects of the VARs sales reporting, auditing, invoicing and revenue collection process. An itemised sales report is provided on a quarterly basis to each member HO, and the revenue generated from these sales is then paid accordingly to each member by IC-ENC.


IC-ENC retains a low cost recovery fee of just $1 per annual equivalent ENC subscription.
